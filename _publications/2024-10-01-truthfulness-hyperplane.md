This work identifies a consistent, universal truthfulness hyperplane present in the internal representation space of multiple large language models (LLMs). This hyperplane can be used to reliably distinguish between truthful and false generations across model families and tasks, enabling lightweight truthfulness detection and mitigation techniques. Code is available at [Universal_Truthfulness_Hyperplane](https://github.com/Vicent0205/Universal_Truthfulness_Hyperplane).
