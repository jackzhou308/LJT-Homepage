This work investigates the core limitations of vision-language models (VLMs) when processing chart data, identifying a critical perception bottleneck that prevents accurate extraction of structural and numerical information from charts. We propose targeted improvements to VLM architecture and training data to address this bottleneck, leading to state-of-the-art performance on chart understanding benchmarks. Code is available at [Vision4Chart](https://github.com/Vicent0205/Vision4Chart).
