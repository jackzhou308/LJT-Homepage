This work presents SynLogic, a framework for generating large-scale, verifiable logical reasoning datasets to enhance the reasoning abilities of large language models. The synthesized data covers diverse logical reasoning tasks and includes formal verification to ensure correctness, leading to improved performance on multiple reasoning benchmarks.
