This work introduces C-Eval, a large-scale evaluation benchmark for foundation models covering 52 diverse disciplines across multiple difficulty levels, from middle school to professional exams, all in Chinese. C-Eval provides a comprehensive way to assess the knowledge and reasoning capabilities of large language models in Chinese contexts, and has become a standard benchmark for evaluating Chinese LLMs.
