This work shows that parameter-efficient modules (PEMs) such as LoRA adapters can be composed using arithmetic operations (addition, subtraction, multiplication) to combine their capabilities, remove unwanted behaviors, or create new task-specific adapters without additional training. This provides a lightweight way to customize large language models for diverse use cases.
